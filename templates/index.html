Established to improve women healthcare

EKC is a five-year project being implemented in partnership with Martin Luther University which is funded by EKFS, to establish One Stop Breast and Cervical Cancer Clinic.

  • Mission

Our Mission

To upgrade and decentralize oncology services, through long-established inter-disciplinary team.

Our Vision

Establishing a one stop service for women with breast or cervical symptoms for timely, high-quality diagnosis and treatment in 2025.

Our Goal

Strengthen the screening and early detection of cervical and breast cancer at EKC-PPCFs

Improve post-screening follow-up and care at EKC-PPCFs

Support the policy environment in the ministry of health

Conduct an implementation research
